About Us

wild rescues is rigistered nonprofit wildlife rescue and rehabilitation facility, located in Ocala, florida. We help injared, sick, neglected, and orphaned wildlife. We help rehabilitate these animals and then release them back into the wild, if possible

Wild Rescue is a member of the National Wildlife Rehabilitators Association.

We help many animals, such as:

  • Raccons
  • Squirrels
  • Fox
  • Birds
  • Horses
  • Deer
  • Pigs
  • Reptiles
